Research

I study how conformal prediction can be wrapped around classical and extended volatility models to produce calibrated, distribution-free prediction intervals for financial time series.

Conformal pipelines for volatility models

ONGOING · KBTU

I am building a unified testing pipeline that evaluates several volatility-forecasting models under a shared conformal-prediction framework, comparing empirical coverage and interval efficiency across regimes.

Models under study:

  • GARCH family — GARCH, EGARCH, GJR-GARCH
  • HAR family — HAR-RV and its extensions
  • Realized-volatility extensions and hybrid ML residual models

The aim is to determine when conformalization meaningfully improves predictive reliability over model-based intervals — and where it fails, particularly under volatility clustering and regime shifts.
