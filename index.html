About

Second-year Data Science student at Kazakh-British Technical University, working at the intersection of statistical learning and applied econometrics.

My ongoing research builds conformal prediction pipelines around classical and extended volatility models — testing GARCH, HAR, and their variants for calibrated, distribution-free interval forecasts on financial time series.

I also work on open-source assistive technology. Our team's project Kozha, a sign language translator, was selected as a finalist at the RESNA Student Design Competition (2026).
