Hi — I'm Zhan, a second-year Data Science student at the [Kazakh-British Technical University](https://kbtu.edu.kz/) in Almaty. My interests sit at the intersection of statistical learning and applied econometrics.

My ongoing research builds **conformal prediction** pipelines around classical and extended volatility models — testing GARCH, HAR, and their variants for calibrated, distribution-free interval forecasts on financial time series. The aim is to determine when conformalization meaningfully improves predictive reliability over model-based intervals, and where it fails — particularly under volatility clustering and regime shifts.

I also work on open-source assistive technology. Our team's project [**Kozha**](/projects/) — a sign language translator — was selected as a finalist at the [RESNA Student Design Competition](https://www.resna.org/) (2026), with a focus on accessibility for Kazakh and Russian Sign Language users.
